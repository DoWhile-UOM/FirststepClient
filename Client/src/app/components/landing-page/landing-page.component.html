What is FirstStep?

FirstStep is a web-based job-matching platform designed to address the specific needs of job seekers and employers. This platform aims to help job seekers find relevant job vacancies, submit application forms securely, and track the status of their applications

FirstStep not only simplifies the application process but also offers real-time application tracking, providing graduates with invaluable insights into their progress and facilitating direct engagement with employers. FirstStep is not just a job search tool; it's a confidence-building companion that empowers graduates to take their crucial first step towards a successful and fulfilling career.

In today's competitive job market, job seekers often face difficulties in finding relevant job opportunities and dealing with complex application processes. Employers also struggle to find suitable candidates efficiently. This platform mainly targets fresh graduates who encounter many challenges in finding their first job. Therefore, it is named FirstStep. We want these fresh graduates to find good jobs that match their skillsets and more, and for employers to hire new talents.
The need for a separate platform to find job vacancies comes when job seekers need to find up-to-date job vacancies that are relevant to their field of work. Additionally, applying for a job can be time-consuming. Job seekers may need to complete forms, write cover letters, and modify resumes for each application. They often receive little to no feedback from employers, even after submitting applications or attending interviews. This platform aims to solve these problems by providing a simple, user-friendly interface for both job seekers and employers. The motivation behind this project is to make the job-matching process more efficient and effective
